📊 The Business Model

How Dropshipping Works

🔄The Dropshipping Flow

Customer orders on your Amazon store → You purchase the same product from a supplier at wholesale → Supplier ships directly to customer → You keep the margin (typically 30-50%)

✅Key Advantages

  • No upfront inventory costs
  • Scalable without storage space
  • Test products with minimal risk
  • Focus on marketing, not logistics
  • Passive income potential

⚠️Realistic Challenges

  • Lower margins than traditional retail
  • Highly competitive market
  • Amazon reviews critical to success
  • Customer service demands
  • Supplier reliability varies

⭐ Gaining Legitimacy (THE KEY)

How New Sellers Build Credibility on Amazon

Problem: New sellers have zero reviews. No reviews = no sales. This playbook solves it.

🎁Strategy 1: Strategic Discounting & Early Buyers

First 30 days: Offer 30-40% discount to first 20-30 customers. Target: 15-20 verified purchases with reviews.

  • Use Amazon "Early Reviewer" program (incentivize honest reviews)
  • Price competitively below incumbents temporarily
  • Goal: 4.5+ star average with 15+ reviews in 30 days

📱Strategy 2: Influencer & Micro-Community Outreach

Parallel to discount phase: Partner with micro-influencers (5K-50K followers) in relevant niches.

  • Offer free/discounted product for honest review (disclosed as such)
  • Target TikTok, Instagram, YouTube reviewers in product category
  • Expected cost: $200-500 for 5-10 influencer reviews + social amplification

🌍Strategy 3: Community Building & Social Proof

Months 1-3: Create social channels and organic customer base.

  • Instagram/TikTok: Show product demos, customer unboxings, lifestyle content
  • Email list: Capture customer emails for future launches and feedback
  • Reddit/Facebook groups: Participate authentically, answer questions
  • Goal: 500+ social followers + engaged email list by month 3

✍️Strategy 4: Authentic Review Requests

Post-purchase follow-up: Professional, helpful communication.

  • Send personalized thank-you notes with QR code to leave review
  • Include free bonus (guide, discount code) unrelated to review incentive
  • Reply thoughtfully to every review (positive & negative) within 24 hours
